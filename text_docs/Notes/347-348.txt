Lines 347-348: She twisted words
One of the examples her father gives is odd. I am quite sure it was I who one day,
when we were discussing "mirror words," observed (and I recall the poet's expression
of stupefaction) that "spider" in reverse is "redips." and "T. S. Eliot," "toilest." But
then it is also true that Hazel Shade resembled me in certain respects.
